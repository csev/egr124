Notes:

    In 1988, the Net was both very mature and very immature. For those with access to the Net it was a powerful effective tool. However the only users were those at the top level universities. The Internet was beginning to move from E-Mail as the primary data transferred to FTP data as the largest amount of bandwidth. Most users were very experienced computer users so the Internet did not have to be particularly user friendly.
