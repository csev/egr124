The WebTV Controversy

  • WebTV is a dial-up (telephone) Internet service

  • Your TV serves as a display monitor

  • WebTV tries to present an integrated and coherent view of the Net

  • Also inserts advertisements on screen while waiting for Web sites you choose to download

  • Several prominent content providers object

    • Note that TV stations have inserted ads in movies for years
    • … but they license the movies for broadcast
