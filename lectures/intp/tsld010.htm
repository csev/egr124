Copyright Basics

  • Under current copyright law any original work is copyrighted

  • Copyright now lasts the life of the author plus 50 years

    • 75 years for non-individual (eg corporate) creators

  • Registration is a way of establishing that the work existed in a particular form

    • Registration is not required for protection
    • May be necessary to recover damages

  • Ie you can violate someone’s copyright even if no copyright notice and/or unregistered
