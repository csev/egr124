Search Engines: Ethical and Copyright Concerns

  • Search engines based on spiders that crawl Web sites and index every word

  • In essence, they build a “concordance” of the Web

  • But a search engine allows a user to skip the “front matter” of a site

  • ... where sites have their own banner ads

  • Consumer gets the content without seeing the ads

  • Is the search engine in effect violating the rights of the copyright holder?
