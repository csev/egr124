Few people have an understanding of what they can and cannot do in the area of copyrights on the Internet. While Richard Wiggins is not an attorney, in this lecture he introduces you to the issues which need to be considered.

Rich Wiggins works on the Network For Excellence in Manufacturing project and was the original webmaster for the MSU Web Page.
