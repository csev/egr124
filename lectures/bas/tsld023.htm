Notes:

    The act of browsing actually consists of retreiving information using the proper protocol, converting that information as necessary, and displaying the information. As the browser “assembles” a complicated page, it may contact 10-20 different computers to retrieve information which makes up the page.
    By clicking on a hyper link, you can “vector-off” to a completely different server in some other part of the country or even a different country.
