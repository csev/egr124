Notes:

    There has been a continuous battle to determine the best browser. At the end of 1995, Netscape was the king of browsers but there were about 30 other browsers which people were actively using. By the beginning of 1997, the competition had whittled down to Netscape and Microsoft Explorer with all of the remaining systems totaling less than 4%. In 1995 nearly 5% of the users were using a text-based browser called Lynx.
