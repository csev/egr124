BASIC TERMINOLOGY

  • HTML (Hyper Text Markup Language) commands are called tags and are enclosed in <>. (Ex: <HTML>)

  • For most commands the tags are paired, one turns the command on, and the other turns it off. (Ex: </CENTER>)

  • The <HTML> tag is not required by most browsers, and neither are the quotes for tag values.

  • Colors are defined by individual RGB codes (Ex: FFFFFF=white, 000000=black); the name of plain colors can also be used instead
