DON’T

  • Use very large graphics

  • Use too many graphics

  • Overuse links embedded in text

  • FLASH! BLINK!

  • Use wallpaper or backgrounds that obliterate your text

  • Have one long page with no links at all

  • Force the viewer to fill out a form or register first (unless you have a fee-based service)

  • Put up anything just to have a presence on the web
