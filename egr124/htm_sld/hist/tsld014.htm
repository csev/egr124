Notes:

    This is an example of a news message announcing the proof of Fermat’s last theorem.
    xn = yn = zn
    Has no integer solutions except for n=1 and n=2
    Fermat was a brilliant mathematician who lived in the 1600’s. After he died, other mathematicians examined his notes and found many brilliant proofs. Before his death he claimed to a colleague, “I have a beautiful and elegant proof to this (the above) theorem”.
    Three hundred years after his death, this was the only of Fermat’s theorems which remained unproved (hence the title “Fermat’s Last Theorem”).
