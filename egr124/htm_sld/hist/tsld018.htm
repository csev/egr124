Notes:

    Gopher was a hierarchy of menus. You navigated through folders and at some point you came upon a sound, text document, or graphic image. The data was retrieved and then displayed or played automatically. As you selected menu choices, you seamlessly moved from one server to another through “gopher-space”.
    Gopher was the first program to integrate indexing, retrieval, conversion, and display of data into a single program.
