Copyright Violations: Civil versus Criminal

  • Copyright violator may be subject to both civil and criminal sanctions

  • Civil:

    • Copyright holder sues you for lost revenue

  • Criminal:

    • Government prosecutes you for criminal violations
    • E.g. you set up a factory making pirated music CDs
    • Note that current law makes it a felony to sell more than 10 copies of copyrighted work w/out permission
