Why Protect Intellectual Property?

  • Copyright

    • Reward creativity, originality
    • Promote many kinds of works: fiction, scholarly writing, newspapers, plays, music -- even TV and films

  • Patents

    • Reward invention
    • New devices, industrial processes, even genetically engineered forms of life

  • Trademarks

    • Allow businesses to build brands
    • Allow consumers to choose favored brands
