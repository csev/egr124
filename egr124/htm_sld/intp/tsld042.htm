Secure Containers

  • Content is digitally encrypted for distribution

  • Metadata -- info about the content -- is in clear text

  • To open the container, a transaction takes place over the Net

  • You must supply correct password (or credit card info, etc) to view

  • Secure viewers prevent cut/copy/paste operations

  • IBM’s Cryptolopes -- main example
