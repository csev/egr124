Concept of “Fair Use”

  • Allows limited copying without permission for

    • Personal use
    • Educational purposes
    • Reviews

  • Educational example: Article in today’s newspaper is relevant to a class you’re teaching

    • It’s fair use to hand out copies; there’d be no time to seek and obtain permission

  • Review examples:

    • Quoting a few lines from a new pop song in a review
    • Printing a thumbnail (tiny) replica of a photograph
