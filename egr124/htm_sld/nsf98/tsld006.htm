    The network-based/shareware approach is great for gadgets. It is horrible for complex systems. Is there yet a good free non-commercial word processor?     Funding is attracted to - “If this gets funded - all poverty, pain, suffering and general unfairness in the world will end. If this does not get funded - a comet will hit earth and we will all be vaporized and die instantly.” Many great innovations come from puttering.
