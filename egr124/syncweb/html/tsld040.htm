Style: To Link or Not To Link

  • Don’t add a link every time a common phrase appears

  • Your pages will look like Christmas trees...

  • ... and will be impossible to read

  • Consider a “Return to Home Page” link on every page

  • Avoid internal links within a single page

    • Very confusing to readers
    • Is it a link to a new page, or to part of this page? Hard to tell..
