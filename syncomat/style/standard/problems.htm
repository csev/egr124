Solving Problems with Viewing the lecture

This page is designed to help you with problems while viewing the lectures. !IFDEF WEB The problems which you may encounter will fall into several categories.
  • It is important to have the proper version of the RealPlayer installed on your computer. !ENDIF !IFDEF CDROM Typically the only problem with viewing from a CD-ROM or local disk is getting the proper version of the RealPlayer installed. !ENDIF You should install the Realplayer available at no charge from www.real.com/products/player/. If you have problems with the G2 Beta player you may want to try the version 5 player. !IFDEF WEB
  • Sluggish network connection - you can pre-cache the slides !IFDEF AUDIOANDVIDEO or switch to Audio-Only view of the lecture !ENDIF
  • You connection is behind a firewall which will not pass the streaming protocol - you can view the lecture using pseudo-streaming or via a direct download.
This document has some solutions for the problems.
  • Slow Network: Pre-Cache Lecture Slides !IFDEF AUDIO !IFDEF VIDEO
  • Slow Network or 486 Computer: Switch to Audio !ENDIF !ENDIF
  • Firewall: !FORPART
    Part P#P: !IFDEF AUDIO
    Pseudo-Streaming Audio     !ENDIF !IFDEF VIDEO
    Pseudo-Streaming Video     !ENDIF !ENDPART
  • Firewall second try (only use on a fast connection) : !FORPART
    Part P#P: !IFDEF AUDIO
    Off-Line Play Audio     !ENDIF !IFDEF VIDEO
    Off-Line Play Video     !ENDIF !ENDPART !ENDIF
