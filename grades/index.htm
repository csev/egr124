The grading in EGR124 is a modified mastery model. Each of the class activities will be graded in a pass/fail mode. Instead of accumulating "points", each student completes assignments. Based on the number of passed assignments, the final grade will be computed. As EGR124 is evolving, the exact details on how grades will be computed will change over time.

Eventually I would like to move toward a model similar to CPS101 at Michigan State University where passing "bridge tasks" moves your grade from 0.0 to 3.0 and a graded project will determine whether or not you get a 3.0, 3.5, or 4.0.

If you have any questions about grading, send me a note at crs@egr.msu.edu.
